About WeChef

Professional training, your kitchen

WeChef was built on a simple belief: every aspiring cook and food entrepreneur deserves access to real, hands-on culinary expertise — right at their own kitchen, their own space.

Led by Chef Antony Jeno Raj S.L, WeChef provides freelance culinary training across Tamil Nadu, Bangalore, and South-Eastern Kerala — personalised to your goals and your business.

  • Freelance culinarians across Tamil Nadu, Bangalore & South-Eastern Kerala
  • Hands-on, practical cooking — learn by doing
  • Beginner-friendly, patient teaching approach
  • Flexible scheduling to suit your timeline
  • Personalised training plans & custom menus
  • Industry-level skills for real-world success
